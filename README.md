# jobfit-ai
JobFit AI is an AI-powered resume analysis and job-matching system that helps job seekers understand how well their resume fits a specific role. Users upload or paste their resume alongside a job description, and the system uses AI to evaluate skill alignment, experience gaps, and overall job fit.
